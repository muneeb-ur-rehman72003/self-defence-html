Meet Soke Browne

Soke Browne is the founder and chief instructor Shodai Soke of the Browne Budo Ryu Kempo jujitsu self-defence system. He has taught Kempo jujitsu in 15 countries and has been inducted into the Combat Hall of Fame, the World Karate Union Hall of Fame, the World Head Founders Hall of Fame and the Martial Arts Masters, Pioneers and Legends Hall of Fame.

Today, he runs jujitsu training classes at four venues in London: Newham, Woolwich, Bermondsey and Pimlico

When you learn from Soke Peter Browne, you’re tapping into a deep well of knowledge and experience.
